Vanilla Cake

Description

Vanilla cake is a light and fluffy dessert made with simple ingredients like flour, sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla extract. It has a delicate and moist crumb that melts in your mouth with each bite. The subtle flavor of vanilla adds a sweet and aromatic taste to the cake, making it a classic and timeless treat loved by many. Whether enjoyed on its own or dressed up with frosting or fruit, vanilla cake is a versatile and crowd-pleasing dessert that is perfect for any occasion. Its simplicity and comforting taste make it a staple in the world of baking.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175(degrees)C.Grease the cake tin with cooking spray and line with parchment paper
  2. Mix flour,sugar,salt and vanilla extract in a large bowl together.
  3. Add eggs and milk to the batter and mix well.
  4. Pour this mix into the greased tin and bake in the preheated oven for about an hour.
  5. Run a table knife around the edges to loosen them.Cool it for 10 mins
